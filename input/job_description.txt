Technology Lead

About Technology

At Huge, technology is not only about depth of expertise, but also about continuous exploration of how technology can create meaningful experiences. Our technologists collaborate closely with strategy, design, and product teams to bring ideas to life. They don’t just build features , they build impactful solutions that address complex client challenges and deliver exceptional digital experiences.

Technology Leads at Huge combine strong engineering fundamentals, leadership, and collaboration to guide teams through complex enterprise implementations while ensuring high-quality, scalable solutions.

Job Description

Huge is looking for a Technology Lead with a strong UI and front-end focus to support the development of a digital platform, primarily built on Adobe Experience Manager (AEM). This role requires a technologist who can lead front-end architecture and implementation, while maintaining an understanding of the full-stack ecosystem in enterprise web environments.

The ideal candidate will have deep experience building high-fidelity web interfaces, strong knowledge of modern web development practices, and experience working with enterprise content management platforms. As a Technology Lead, you will collaborate with designers, strategists, and developers to deliver scalable and production-ready solutions.

What You’ll Be Doing

Lead the development and implementation of high-quality UI components and front-end architectures.
Implement high-fidelity front-end designs, ensuring pixel accuracy and performance across devices and browsers.
Guide the development team in best practices for web development, architecture, and code quality.
Work across the full web stack, collaborating with backend teams and integrating front-end solutions with enterprise platforms.
Contribute to the implementation and integration of Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) solutions.
Apply modern front-end development practices, including modular architecture and build tooling.
Lead and participate in code reviews, ensuring maintainable, scalable, and performant code.
Collaborate with UX designers, product teams, and engineers to deliver seamless digital experiences.
Identify technical risks, architectural gaps, and potential improvements early in the development process.
Support the team in delivering production-ready web applications and digital platforms.
Mentor engineers and provide technical guidance to ensure consistent engineering quality across the team.
What We’d Like to See

5+ years of experience in web development, including experience in senior engineer or technical lead roles.
Strong expertise in front-end web development, including:
HTML5
CSS3
JavaScript
Experience with modern front-end build tools, especially Webpack.
Experience delivering web platforms that have successfully launched to production.
Experience working with Enterprise Content Management or Experience Management platforms.
Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) experience is highly desirable.
Experience with Java or familiarity with Java-based platforms.
Familiarity with modern front-end frameworks, particularly React.
Experience with TypeScript is a plus.
Experience with Sass / SCSS or other CSS preprocessors.
Understanding of full-stack web architecture and integration patterns.
Experience working in enterprise or agency environments is a plus.
This role is currently not available for hire or work in Colorado, New Mexico, and Hawaii.
